What is Digital Sentinel?

Digital Sentinel is a free, open-source toolkit that demystifies cybersecurity. It provides clear, actionable intelligence on your passwords and digital habits. Founded on a "privacy-by-design" principle, every tool runs locally in your browser. No data ever leaves your device.

Key Features

Strength Analysis

Get an instant score on your password's complexity, length, and predictability based on entropy calculations.

Privacy-First Breach Check

Securely check if your password has appeared in known data breaches using a k-Anonymity model that protects your privacy.

AI-Powered Advice

Receive personalized, context-aware security advice from a local AI to improve your digital hygiene.

Privacy-First Architecture

1. Local Analysis

All analysis runs inside your browser. Nothing is sent to a server.

2. Anonymized Check

Only a tiny, anonymous part of your password's hash is used for breach checks (k-Anonymity).

3. Local AI Advice

The analysis results are fed to a local AI model (like Ollama) to generate your private, personalized advice.
